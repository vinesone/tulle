Flow layout: sources are boxes that pack inline (left-to-right, wrapping) until wrapped in a block(), which stacks its children and breaks the line. A badge is placed absolute against the frame. Everything is solved in design-space pixels and re-solved every frame, so drag the sliders and watch it re-flow live — the renderer only ever sees the resulting transforms. The badge bobs and pulses: its offset and opacity are wave()/ keyframes() functions of time, resolved every frame.
